Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet. Scientists believe that water once existed on its surface, making it one of the most important planets for future exploration and possible human life.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. It has a massive storm called the Great Red Spot and more than 90 moons orbiting it.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its rings made of ice and rock. It is one of the most visually stunning planets.
